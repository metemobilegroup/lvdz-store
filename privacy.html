Children’s Privacy (COPPA & GDPR Compliance)

Our applications are designed for children. We strictly comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We do not collect, request, or store any personal information from children under the age of 13. To ensure a safe environment, we implement a mandatory Parental Gate before allowing access to external links or commercial transactions (In-App Purchases).

Sharing of Information

We do not provide personally identifiable data (like names or phone numbers) to external companies. Non-personal technical information is only shared with the third-party providers listed in this policy to ensure app functionality, performance monitoring, and contextual advertising in compliance with children's privacy regulations.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party SDKs configured strictly for the Kids Category. These services are set to non-behavioral mode to ensure no tracking of minors:

  • AdMob (Google): Advertisements are restricted to the "G" (General) rating. We utilize the tagForChildDirectedTreatment flag. Google provides human review of ad creatives. Policies: Human Review and G-Rating.
  • AppLovin: Configured for children's privacy compliance (COPPA). Policy: https://www.applovin.com/privacy/
  • Unity: Used for technical features and/or contextual advertising in non-behavioral mode. Policy: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Firebase & Crashlytics: Used only for anonymous performance monitoring and crash reports.
  • Cloudflare (R2 Storage): Used to host and deliver app assets (audio/images). Cloudflare may process basic technical logs (such as IP addresses) to ensure security and efficient delivery. Policy: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/

We do not track or utilize the precise geographic location of your device.
